Help & FAQ

Your questions answered – all in one place.

1. How do I sign up for SheReads?

Click the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner and fill in your details to create an account.

2. Is SheReads free to use?

Yes! All the digital resources and services provided by SheReads are free and accessible after logging in.

3. How can I access eBooks?

Go to the “Services” menu and click on “eBooks”. You'll be redirected to our digital bookshelf to start reading instantly.

4. What is Koha Web-OPAC?

Koha is our integrated online catalog system where you can browse our digital library collection.

5. Who can I contact for support?

You can reach out via shereadss25@gmail.com or visit the Contact Us page.
